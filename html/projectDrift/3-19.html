Player Smooth Attack Stepping

In order to implement a nice looking smooth attack, the player should have the functionality to attack step. Attack step before was a 1 frame teleportation in the direction of attack. I reworked the system to be smooth and adjustable by design using Unity AnimationCurves, as programmed in a way that is reusable by other systems (namely flurry attacks). This took a while to figure out. Logic was originally in the AttackController, which means each step will always be the same but it was subsequently moved to the weapon as weapons may require different step lengths.
